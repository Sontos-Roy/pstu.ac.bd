Research Interest

Subject Description Research Interest (Goal, Target Indicator)
Polymer Chemistry

Extraction of lignin from lignocellulosic biomasses, their modification and characterization.
Electrochemistry

Study of interaction between different metals and ligands in solution using various electrochemical techniques. Detection and analysis of organic isomers using CV and DPV.
Coordination Chemistry

Preparation characterization and studies of physical and chemical properties of coordination compounds of biologically important metal ions and ligands.
