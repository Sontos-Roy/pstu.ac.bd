Dr. Md. Shahidul Islam was awarded Ph.D. 2002 in Mathematical Biology and MS in Dynamical Systems from Georg-August-Universität Göttingen, Germany in 1999. He also completed M.Sc. (Pure Mathematics) and B.Sc. (Hons) from the University of Dhaka in 1992 and 1989 in secured first class first position in B.Sc. After completing his M.Sc., he joined Shah Jalal University of Science and Technology (SUST) Sylhet in 1992 as a lecturer and joined the University of Dhaka in 1994. He visited France, Netherland, Nepal, Myanmar, and India to attend the conferences. He has been published more than 50 papers in reputed national and international journals. He is the President of the Bangladesh Mathematical Society (BMS) and had been served as Vice president, Secretary, Treasurer, and executive member of the Society last sixteen years. He is a Life Member of the Alumni Association of German Universities in Bangladesh, Bangladesh Academy Sciences (BAS), Association for the Advancement of Science (BAAS), Dhaka University Mathematics Alumni Association, Registrar Graduate of Dhaka University and Bangladesh Mathematical Society (BMS), etc.

He organized several International and National Mathematics Conferences and workshops in the Department of Mathematics, University of Dhaka. He was the 1st Local Coordinator of CIMPA Research School on Dynamical Systems and its Application to Biology in the Department of Mathematics, University of Dhaka held on 10 – 21 June 2019.

He was the Convener of International Day of Mathematics (IDM) Celebration Committee 2020 - 2022, 1st organizing secretary of the National Mathematics Conference of A F Mujibur Rahman - Bangladesh Mathematical Society (BMS), and organized a series of the training program for college and school Mathematics teachers supported by CASIO organized by Bangladesh Mathematical Society.

He is the Director, IQAC of the department of Mathematics, DU.

He supervised 3 Ph.D., 2 M.Phil, 32 MS, and 63 honors students.

He is acting as a Chairman of the department.
