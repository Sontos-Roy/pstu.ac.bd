Research Interest

Subject Description Research Interest (Goal, Target Indicator)
Inter-Religious Peace and Harmony

Inter-Religious peace and harmony are essential for a developed, civilized and prosperous society. It is time to ensure peaceful coexistence of all in Bangladesh irrespective of race, religion and caste. Research on Inter-Religious peace and harmony is needed to effectively implement this demand. However, the reality is that there is very little research in this regard in our country. To meet this need, I have chosen to study Inter-Religious peace and harmony.
Contemporary Contexts and Islam

Islam is a complete system of life. Islam has beautiful, timely and scientific solutions to the issues that are constantly happening in our lives and society. Islam always speaks of truth, beauty, justice and welfare and guarantees all the rights of the world humanity. The aim of my research in this regard is to highlight the various issues existing in our society in the light of Islam. The main objective of this research is to explore the real welfare of man, humanity and human civilization.
Comparative Religions, World Religions

Comparative religion and world religion is a widely discussed topic in today's world. In this world of many religions and many philosophies, the study and research of this subject is very important in bringing diversity in human life. If the essence of religions is applied in human society, then that society can be of peace, friendship and humanity. Comparative religion and world religion research is a very effective subject for a liberal, tolerant and humane society.
