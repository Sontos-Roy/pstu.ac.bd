Tasneem Siddiqui is the Chairperson of Department of Political Science, University of Dhaka and Founding Chair of the Refugee and Migratory Movements Research Unit (RMMRU). Her work on climate change adaptation and migration and labour migration have been published in various journals. She led the drafting of the National Strategy for Internal Displacement in Bangladesh 2021 and Action Plan 2022, the Overseas Employment Policy 2006 and a committee member of the Migration Act 2013. She is in the Global Editorial Board of Journal of Migration Studies, Oxford University, a member, Advisory Committee of the Platform on Disaster Displacement and the Board of Trustees of Transparency International Bangladesh.
