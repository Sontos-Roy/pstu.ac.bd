Research Interest

Subject Description Research Interest (Goal, Target Indicator)
Electrical Bioimpedance

Instrumentation for multi-frequency impedance measurement systems, 3D Sensitivity analysis of bioimpedance measurement techniques, FEM modeling and simulation of bioimpedance measurements, Impedance Spectroscopy, Focused Impedance Method and its application in medicine: specifically tumour characterization, monitoring anatomical structures and physiological events.
Biomedical Signal and Image Analysis

Biomedical signal and image analysis for disease diagnosis, Machine Learning, Transfer Learning, Bangla sign language interpretation, driver drowsiness detection to prevent accidents.
