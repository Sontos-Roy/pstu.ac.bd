Research Interest

Subject Description Research Interest (Goal, Target Indicator)
Historiography

The art of writing history particularly that of Arab Khilafat and Indo-Muslim
Minority Studies

History of Myanmar (Burma) and the Origin and Development of Rakhine community, British Muslim Communities etc.
Emergence of Bangladesh and Bangladesh Studies

Freedom movements before the Liberation War and verious aspects of the War of Independence of Bangladesh

Urbanization

History of Urbanization in Medieval India and Bengal. The title of Ph.D dissertation was- The Socia Life and Urban Centres in Eastern India, 13th and 14th Centuries

Socio-Cultural and Economic History

Socio-Cultural and Economic History of the Muslim world during medieval and modern era with special focus on India and Bengal
