Afroza Shelley has completed her Doctor of Engineering from the department of Nuclear Engineering, Research Laboratory for Nuclear Reactors, Tokyo Institute of Technology, Japan. She also pursued three years post doctoral fellowship in the Laboratory for Advanced Reactor Design, Department of Energy Science, Japan Atomic Energy Research Institute. Her research interest on Neutronic analysis of Rooppur Nuclear Power Plant (VVER-1200 Reactor) and Light Water Reactor, Advanced Fuel for Light Water Reactors, Advanced Reactor Design, Spent fuel anlyses and Radiotoxicity Hazard Calculations.
