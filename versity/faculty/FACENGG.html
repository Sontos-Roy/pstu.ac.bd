Introduction to the faculty

The Faculty of Engineering and Technology set out on its journey on June 26, 2008 in accordance with the first ordinance of the Dhaka University Order, 1973, and the approval of the senate on the recommendation of the academic council and syndicate. This faculty was established to produce skilled manpower in the engineering and technology fields who can effectively contribute to developing innovative, creative, and sustainable solutions for the industrial development of the country. At the time of commencement, the Faculty of Engineering and Technology had 3 departments under its supervision: Applied Physics, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Applied Chemistry & Chemical Engineering, and Computer Science & Engineering.
