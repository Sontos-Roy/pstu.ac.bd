About Department of Islamic Studies

A Brief Introduction to the Department of Islamic Studies The Department of Islamic Studies is a prestigious department at the University of Dhaka. It is one of the few departments with which the University came into existence. It was once called the ‘Corner Stone’ of the University. Initially, it was coupled with the Arabic, but later on, emerged as an independent department. The Vision of the Department is disseminating Islamic education and producing virtuous citizens for the country. Over the years, it produced many world-renowned Islamic thinkers.
