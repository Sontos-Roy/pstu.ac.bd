About Department of Banking and Insurance

The vision of the Department is to develop skilled human resources with specialized education and training particularly in Banking and Financial services. Since its inception in 2004, the Department is committed to catering the needs of modern business world and producing executives and trained manpower with strong skills in business, particularly in banking and insurance. The Department offers 4 year BBA program, 1 year MBA program and other post-graduate programs (including M.Phil. & Ph.D.) in Department. It has introduced MBA (Evening) program for the executives and officials.
