About Department of Educational and Counselling Psychology

Department of Educational and Counselling Psychology emerged as an independent department in 2011. It offers professional postgraduate higher degree (Masters, MPhil and PhD) in Educational Psychology and Counselling Psychology. The department is committed to build skilled manpower to deliver ethical and standard psychological services for ensuring mental health of the country.
