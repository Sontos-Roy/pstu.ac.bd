About Department of Management

Department of Management University of Dhaka Dhaka-1000 A Brief Profile of the Department The Department of Management commenced its journey in 1970. Currently Department offers four years undergraduate BBA program, one year MBA program under the current syllabus. The Department is also offering M.Phil and Ph.D and DBA degrees. Additionally, the Department is offering MBA (Evening) and Master of Professional Human Resource Management (MPHRM). There are 30 teachers in the Department, of which 12 are Professors, 2 Associate Professors, 14 Assistant Professors and 2 lecturers.
