About Department of Clinical Psychology

The Department of Clinical Psychology is the only one of its kind in Bangladesh offering postgraduate studies (MS, MPhil, PhD) and training in clinical psychology at international standards and producing professional Clinical Psychologists for the country. With tireless efforts of the late Professor Anisur Rahman and support from Dr. Graham E. Powell, a practicing British clinical psychologist, this postgraduate professional training program was initiated as a new stream at the Department of Psychology in 1996 under a link project between the University of London and the University of Dhaka.
